RedFlow AI is an explainable deep learning framework designed to enhance clinical decision support by predicting blood transfusion needs and estimating the optimal blood quantity required. Our AI-driven system improves hospital resource planning, patient outcomes, and blood bank management in Nigeria and beyond.
